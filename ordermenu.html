About us

Founded in 1960, Domino’s has grown into the world’s largest pizza company, serving millions of customers in over 90 countries. In India, Domino’s opened its first store in 1996 and now operates more than 1,200 restaurants across 276 cities.

Our mission is simple: deliver hot, delicious pizza quickly and reliably. With innovations like the 30-minute delivery guarantee and real-time order tracking, Domino’s continues to make pizza ordering easy and enjoyable.
